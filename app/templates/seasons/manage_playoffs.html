How Playoff Resolution Works

  1. Seeds - Assign teams to seeds (Seed 1, Seed 2, etc.) based on regular season standings
  2. Bracket Positions - These are filled in as playoff games are played
  3. Schedule Games - When scheduling playoff games, use "Seed 1 vs Seed 4" etc.
  4. Resolve Winners - After each game, set the winner to fill the next bracket position

Tip: You can schedule games against seeds before assigning teams. Once you resolve "Seed 1" to an actual team, games scheduled against "Seed 1" will show the real team name.
